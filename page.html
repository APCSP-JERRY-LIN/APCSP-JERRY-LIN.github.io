Qustions and Answer
Every summer and winter vacation, the school will organize online supplementary courses. If you fail the final exam, you can choose to enroll in an online remedial credit course. It's voluntary, but it also requires money. It is important that you are not given a diploma if you do not have the necessary credits when you graduate.

AP Exam
Replies to (AP exam) AP Calculus test will be held on May 8. The bus will arrive at the school gate at 6:30 and leave for the test site at 6:40 on the dot without calling the roll. Please arrive at the test site one hour earlier. If you miss the bus, please go there by yourself. Please read the instructions carefully again and bring your test equipment (pencil, eraser, pencil, calculator).

The following four things are emphasized:
1. Id card
2. 48-hour nucleic acid test paper version report (as for the afternoon test, please prepare two copies, zhejiang Office or Alipay can be)
3. Admission ticket (above 2, 3 is to receive, each must prepare a copy. Please type by yourself.

Other information:
After entering the hotel, there are two elevators on the right side, go directly to the second floor. About 50 minutes before the test, a teacher will come out and ask you to check your ID card, admission ticket and stationery, etc.
(The second floor is a relatively small platform, it is suggested to put your bag early, otherwise there will be no place for you to put it).
